Privacy policy

Your data, handled with the same care as your beads.

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Aura Club ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why, in plain language.

What we collect

  • Order details — your name, shipping address, email, and what you bought, so we can make and send your order.
  • Payment info — processed securely by our payment provider. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Optional info — an email you give us for the newsletter, or messages you send through the contact form.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymised analytics about how the site is used, to help us improve it.

How we use it

  • To process, make, and ship your orders
  • To reply to your questions and provide support
  • To send updates or offers — only if you opted in (unsubscribe anytime)
  • To keep the store secure and running smoothly

Who we share it with

Only the partners needed to run the shop — for example our payment processor and shipping carrier — and only the minimum they need. We never sell your personal information.

Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to remember your cart and preferences, plus optional analytics cookies. You can clear these anytime in your browser settings.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Just contact us and we'll take care of it.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally; the date above always reflects the latest version.
