Not your vibe? No drama. Here's the 30-day deal.

Last updated: 3 July 2026

The 30-day vibe guarantee

If your bracelet isn't giving what it was supposed to give, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the item price.

What qualifies

  • Item is unworn and in its original condition
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • You have your order number or confirmation email

How to start a return

  • Email us via the contact page with your order number and the reason.
  • We'll reply with a simple return address and instructions within a business day.
  • Pop the item back in protective packaging and send it our way.
  • Once it arrives and passes a quick check, we refund your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Damaged or wrong item

If something arrived damaged or we sent the wrong piece, that's on us — message us within 7 days with a photo and we'll make it right with a free replacement or refund, return shipping included.

A few honest notes

  • Return shipping for change-of-mind returns is covered by the buyer.
  • Custom-sized pieces are made just for you and may not be eligible unless faulty.
  • Natural stones vary slightly in colour and veining — that's a feature, not a defect.
