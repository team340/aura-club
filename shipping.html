Shipping ✦

How your good vibes get from our table to your wrist.

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Processing time

Every bracelet is handmade to order, so we need a little time to string yours. Orders are made and dispatched within 1–2 business days. During big drops or holidays it may take an extra day — we'll always email you if anything changes.

Shipping rates

  • Free shipping on all orders over $50.
  • Orders under $50 ship at a flat $5 rate.
  • Rates are shown clearly at checkout before you pay — no surprises.

Delivery estimates

Once shipped, typical delivery windows are:

  • Domestic: 3–6 business days
  • International: 7–18 business days, depending on your location and customs

These are estimates from the carrier, not guarantees. Delays outside our control (weather, customs, postal backlogs) can happen.

Tracking

You'll get a confirmation email when your order ships, with a tracking link where available. Keep an eye on your spam folder just in case.

International orders & customs

Any import duties, taxes, or customs fees are set by your country and are the buyer's responsibility. We can't predict or cover these.

Wrong address?

Please double-check your shipping details at checkout. If you spot a mistake, contact us right away and we'll try to fix it before your order ships.

Questions we didn't answer? Our FAQ and contact page have you covered.
