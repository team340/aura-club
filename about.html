Made by hand, worn with intention

Aura Club is a tiny studio with a big belief: the little things you wear can carry real meaning.

How it started

A bowl of beads & a big feeling.

It began at a kitchen table, restringing bracelets late at night because the ones in stores felt hollow — plasticky stones, zero soul.

So we started making our own: real gemstones, chosen one at a time, strung slowly. Friends wanted them. Then friends of friends. Aura Club is what grew from that bowl of beads — still handmade, still one order at a time.
