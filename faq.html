Good questions ✦

Everything you might want to know before (and after) you buy.

Are the stones real gemstones?
Yes — every bracelet uses genuine polished gemstone beads, never plastic or glass imitations. Because they're natural, colour and veining vary slightly from bead to bead. That's the good part.
What size are the bracelets? Will it fit me?
They're strung on durable stretch cord at roughly 16–20cm, which comfortably fits most wrists. Measure snugly around your wrist and add about 1cm for comfort. Need a custom size? Message us before ordering.
How long until it ships?
Each piece is handmade to order and ships within 1–2 business days. Delivery time after that depends on your location and chosen method. Full details on our shipping page.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes ✦ shipping is free on all orders over $50. Under that, a small flat rate applies at checkout.
What's your return policy?
If it's not your vibe, return it within 30 days for a full refund. Items should be unworn and in original condition. See returns for the how-to.
How do I care for my bracelet?
Keep natural stones away from perfume, lotion, and long soaks (pool, shower, ocean). Wipe gently with a soft dry cloth. Treated kindly, they'll stay shiny for years.
Do crystals actually *do* anything?
We share the traditional meanings because they're beautiful and fun — think of them as daily reminders and good intentions, not medical or scientific claims. Wear them for the vibe, not a prescription. ✦
Can I cancel or change my order?
Since we make to order fast, reach out within a couple of hours of ordering and we'll do our best. Once it's strung and shipped we can't change it, but returns still apply.
